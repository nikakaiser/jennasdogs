Jenna Nicole Mourey (born: September 15, 1986), better known online as JennaMarbles, is an American YouTube celebrity, vlogger, comedian, internet personality, and blogger. "Jenna Marbles" is a pseudonym incorporating her dog, Mr. Marbles' name. Her first viral video was "How to Trick People Into Thinking You're Good Looking," which gained over 5.3 million views in its first week posted. Jenna Marbles is one of the most popular YouTube personalities. She is the 106th most subscribed YouTube channel in the world and the 39th most subscribed channel from the United States. She is the most subscribed to female YouTuber in the United States, and the second most subscribed to female YouTuber in the world following Mexican YouTuber, Yuya.
